Thoughts about the author

Overall, Oscar Wilde created many works, not only in literature but in drama and theatre. In my opinion Wilde’s “The Picture of Dorian Gray” was an intricate work of fiction with meanings relatable to real life, and was a great novel, written in turn by a great author.
